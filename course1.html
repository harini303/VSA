Learning Flow in VSA

For our VSA students, not even a single day goes without learning. With a proper mixture of theoretical learning, hands-on sessions, assignments and special sessions makes learning very engaging and effective. Have a look at how your learning flow looks in VSA.

1

Live Classes

Mentors will be taking you through live Live classes by explaining concepts and its real-time use-case demonstration. Interact and build a sound understanding of concepts on a daily basis.

2

Structured Interventions

Apply your learnings from Live Classes by creating assignments and projects on your own. This will be facilitated by structured online intervention sessions with our mentors which include assignment/project explainer and its expected output.

3

Unlimited Doubt Clearing

When you actually start coding you will get stuck somewhere or get doubts. Use our learning platform to clarify your doubts from 9 AM to 7 PM with our mentors.

4

Project Execution

Start working on project implementation as per the requirements given by the mentor. There will be intermediate checkpoints which you need to complete and show your output.

5

Project Review and Feedback

Every learning activity in VSA comes with a submission deadline. You are supposed to submit them via online with our mentors in order to kick-start your evaluation. The same happens for projects also.

6

Workshops & Follow-ups

There will be a bunch of actions happening under the as live classes. This includes resume preparation, interview-facing tips, subject-specific prep sessions to crack your interviews.
